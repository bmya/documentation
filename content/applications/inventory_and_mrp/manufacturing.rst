=============
Manufacturing
=============

**Odoo Manufacturing** helps your schedule, plan and process manufacturing orders. With
the workcenter control panel, put tablets in the shop floor to control all your work orders
in real time and allow workers to trigger maintenance operations, feedback loops, quality
issues, etc.
