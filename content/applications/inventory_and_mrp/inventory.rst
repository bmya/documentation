=========
Inventory
=========

**Odoo Inventory** is both an inventory applications, and a warehouse management system, with an
advanced barcode scanner app. In these guides, you will learn how ot manage lead times, how
to automate replenishments, and configure advanced routes like drop-shipping, cross-docks, etc.

   - `Inventory Tutorials <https://www.odoo.com/slides/inventory-24>`_
   - `Barcode Scanner Tutorials <https://www.odoo.com/slides/barcode-30>`_
